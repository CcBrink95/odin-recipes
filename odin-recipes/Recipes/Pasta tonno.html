Pasta Tonno

Description

A healthy and tasty recipe that you can whip up easily after a long day, full of vegetables.

    Wheat Pasta
    Cherry Tomatoes
    Tuna
    Zucchini
    Olives
    Pesto
    First, boil water for the pasta
    Second slice the vegetables to preferred sizes
    Third cook vegetables until they are done
    Add Olives
    Add cooked pasta and pesto
    Stir
    Done!
